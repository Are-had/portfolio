First Year Research Report

Geophysical Validation of EarthCARE/ATLID Lidar

A comprehensive report detailing the methodology developed at LMD to validate the ATLID satellite instrument. The study focuses on comparing satellite backscatter signals with ground-based measurements from the IPRAL lidar (SIRTA observatory) to characterize stratospheric aerosols and calibrate the data pipeline.
